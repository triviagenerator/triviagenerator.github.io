Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

This is the Cookie Policy for Random Trivia Generator. Like most interactive platforms, our ecosystem uses small text fragments and browser features to streamline user interaction.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are tiny files that are downloaded to your computer or device to improve your browsing experience. This page describes what information they gather, how we use them, and why we sometimes need to store these cookies.

2. How We Use Cookies and Storage

We use technology elements for several simple reasons:

  • Scoreboard Memory: We use your web browser's local state structures to tally how many trivia questions you have been asked, how many you answered correctly, and your overall accuracy percentage. This data is not synchronized with a central system and stays completely inside your device.
  • Preferences Tracking: Storing selected category options or chosen filter states so that hitting refreshing configurations won't break your customized trivia settings.

3. Third-Party Cookie Layers

In some special cases, we also use cookies provided by trusted third parties. The following section details which third-party elements you might encounter through this site:

  • This site may use Google Analytics, which is one of the most widespread and trusted web analytics solutions, to help us understand how you use the site and ways that we can improve your trivia experience. These tracking metrics may track things such as how long you spend on the site and the trivia channels you visit.

4. Disabling Cookies

You can prevent the setting of cookies by adjusting the settings on your browser (see your browser Help menu for instructions on how to do this). Be aware that disabling cookies might affect the functionality of this and many other websites that you visit. Disabling cookies on this platform will usually result in resetting your scoreboard counts every single time you navigate away.
